Eco-Friendly Homes with Rainwater Harvesting

Explore residential areas where homes utilize rainwater harvesting systems to conserve water, reduce utility costs, and contribute to a more sustainable lifestyle. These homes are designed with eco-friendly features to benefit both residents and the environment.

Featured Residential Areas with Rainwater Harvesting

Green Meadows Estate

Location: 123 Green Lane, Meadowville

Size: 4,000 sq. ft. (per house)

Rainwater Harvesting Features: Roof collection system, filtration and purification system for irrigation and non-potable uses.

Price: $2,500/month (rent)

Description: Green Meadows Estate offers spacious homes with rainwater harvesting systems to provide a sustainable water source for gardening, landscaping, and even home sanitation. This helps reduce water bills while supporting an eco-friendly lifestyle.

Eco Villas

Location: 456 Eco Rd, Green Valley

Size: 3,500 sq. ft. (per villa)

Rainwater Harvesting Features: Rainwater storage tanks, high-efficiency irrigation for gardens, eco-friendly rooftop garden irrigation.

Price: $3,200/month (rent)

Description: Eco Villas are luxurious homes that incorporate advanced rainwater harvesting techniques. The water collected from the roof is filtered and used for irrigation, reducing the villa's dependency on city water and promoting water conservation.

Sunnydale Green Residences

Location: 789 Sun Valley, Hilltop

Size: 5,200 sq. ft. (per house)

Rainwater Harvesting Features: Comprehensive rainwater collection system, filtration for potable and non-potable uses, drought-resistant landscaping.

Price: $3,800/month (rent)

Description: Sunnydale Green Residences are designed to minimize environmental impact. Each home features a rainwater harvesting system that supplies water for both household use and garden irrigation, significantly lowering water consumption and fostering a greener lifestyle.
